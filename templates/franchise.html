Franchise Power Rankings


Rank Team Comments
1 Cole This one was the easiest. Cole is the only member with 2 championships. Over the years, he has been a little boom or bust- either a getting playoff bye or missing them completely. However the rings speak for themselves.
2 Dooley Outside of Cole, Dooley is the only member have a ring and appear in multiple finals. Dooley's down years have been especially bad, but his postseason success has more than matched up than most others and was one week away from being on the same level as Cole.
3 Tony Plush Tony Plush has a championship (albiet a long time ago). He's also a mainstay in the playoffs only missing it once in the 7 years of the league. The only two players more consistent than him are Falco and David Ball's but neither have a ring.
4 David's Balls While Nat doesn't have a ring, he's had a first round bye in every year of his short career in Milton fantasy, including a trip to the finals. It's hard to rank him any higher because he doesn't have a ring yet; however he's been nothing short of a force over his short tenure in the league. The criteria of the franchise power rankings tend to hurt newer teams, but it is impressive he already this high in the franchise power rankings.
5 Matty Ice Matty Ice has been mediocre for the majority of his career. Over the last 4 years he's made the playoffs dispite being sub .500 and was able to turn one of those playoff berths into a championship. Matty Ice has failed to produce any powerhouse teams over the years- just a bunch of medicore teams and one happened to get hot at right time.
6 Falcon Falcone has been the most consistent manager with multiple trips to the finals. The major blemish on his resume is the lack of a championship. A championship would vault him up in the franchise power rankings, due to then fact he's always in contention. He will get a bump in the preseason power rankings most likely, but his lack of a ring will anchor him lower in the franchise power rankings.
7 Bucci Once upon a time, Bucci was a powerhouse, with a championship and then a first round bye the next year. Since then, a once proud program has been lacking. He does though, have a championship which is more than some other managers can say.
8 David David had a hot start to his time in the league, with a playoff appearance in his rookie year. Since then, however, he has failed to recapture that first year magic.
9 SZN WHile SZN showed promise last year, he only has one playoff win to show for in 4 years. He's the only member of the league to have never appeared in the finals.
10 Stocktown Stocktown's championship year is tainted with ~alleged~ collusion. Outside of that Stocktown doesn't have a playoff win.
